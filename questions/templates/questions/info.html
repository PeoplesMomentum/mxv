    Candidate Q&A

    This is your chance to find out more about the Momentum members running in the leadership election. You can ask a question, upvote questions submitted by other members and read candidate’s responses.

    How do I ask a question?

    Every member can submit one question on one of the topics listed below. Questions can be upvoted by other members and popular questions will rise to the top. All questions are moderated to make sure they aren’t repetitive and are in line with Momentum’s code of ethics.

    Question examples

    Winning Elections for Labour

    Q: ‘What do you think went wrong in the 2019 election?’

    Build a social movement

    Q: ‘How will you help Momentum members organise in their workplaces?’

    Build a sustainable organisation

    Q: ‘How will you develop our ability to raise funds?’

    Transform the Labour Party

    Q: ‘How will you campaign to make MPs accountable to the membership?’

    Train activists and build new leadership at all levels of the party

    Q: ‘What are your plans for political education?’

    Other key political priorities

    Q: ‘Do you think protesters were right to pull down the statue of Edward Colston in Bristol?’

    Candidate Q&A

    This is your chance to find out more about the Momentum members running for in the leadership election. You can ask a question, upvote questions submitted by other members and read candidate’s responses.

    How do I ask a question?

    Every member can submit one question on one of the topics listed below. Questions can be upvoted by other members and popular questions will rise to the top. All questions are moderated to make sure they aren’t repetitive and are in line with Momentum’s code of ethics.

    Question examples

    Winning Elections for Labour

    Example question: "What do you think went wrong in the 2019 election?"

    Build a social movement

    Example question: "How will you help Momentum members organise in their workplaces?"

    Build a sustainable organisation

    Example question: "How will you develop our ability to raise funds?"

    Transform the Labour Party

    Example question: "How will you campaign to make MPs accountable to the membership?"

    Train activists and build new leadership at all levels of the party

    Example question: "What are your plans for political education?"

    Other key political priorities

    Example question: "Do you think protesters were right to pull down the statue of Edward Colston in Bristol?"

    Answer rules

    Candidates can answer any questions they want and in any order. All answers will be moderated to ensure they comply with our candidate code of conduct

    Technical help

    Momentum members will receive an email from mymomentum@peoplesmomentum.com containing a single-use activation link. Simply click on this link to start the activation of your My Momentum account, set a password and start using My Momentum.

    For technical issues please email: mymomentum@peoplesmomentum.com


    Labour’s Democracy Review

    Momentum have developed a digital platform to generate ideas for submissions to the Labour Party Democracy Review, and allow members to comment on and upvote proposals they support. The aim is to enable a transparent debate between Momentum members, broaden our knowledge and discussion about party reform and ensure that Momentum’s submissions to the Democracy Review come from the grassroots of the movement.

    Please see our guide on how the platform works, or our more detailed FAQs.

Constituency Labour Party Governance

SUMMARY: Labour should reform the governance of Constituency Labour Parties (CLPs). Key points include:

  • CLP Executives to always include an Online Communications Officer, a Political Education and Training Officer and a Trade Union Liaison Officer (TULO), with full voting rights.
  • CLP Executives to always include representatives of BAME, LGBTQ and disabled people, with full voting rights, or a combined equalities officer if that isn’t possible.
  • CLP Executives to allow job shares for positions.
  • The Labour Party to increase funding to CLPs.
  • Efforts to ensure that all members of the CLP Exec are fully informed of guidance notes for CLPs produced by Disability Equalities Act Labour to improve accessibility.

Source of proposal: This is based in part on proposals by the Members' Council, Caroline, Lydia, Andy, Ian, Jessica, as well as Heather on behalf of the Campaign for Labour Party Democracy.

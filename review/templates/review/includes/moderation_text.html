Posts or comments that breach Momentum's Code of Ethics, or are not relevant to the Democracy Review's remit, will be removed.

Please only click ‘Request moderation’ if you think it might be in contravention of the Moderation Policy, on the grounds of being abusive, irrelevant, or problematic in some other way. Moderation requests are only visible to the member that requests them, and the moderator. To express disagreement with a proposal, please use the comments function. If the moderator thinks that someone has flagged a proposal for moderation when in fact they are simply making a comment, they may turn it into a comment.
